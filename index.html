About

A small kitchen, a specific idea.

Dada opened in Phillip with a simple aim — to cook modern Asian food with the same care a pastry chef brings to a tart. House-aged kimchi, twenty-four-hour broths, ceremonial-grade matcha, a dessert program treated as seriously as the savoury side.

We source locally where it makes sense, and from further afield where the difference is real. Most of what arrives on the plate was made the same day, from raw ingredients, by people who care about it.
